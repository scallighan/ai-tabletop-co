# Kingdoms Ascendant Expansion Pack - Review

**Rating:** ★★★★★
**Reviewer:** Marcus T.
**Date:** March 28, 2026

We thought the base game of Kingdoms Ascendant was already amazing, but this expansion takes it to a whole new level. The new siege mechanics add a layer of strategy that makes every turn feel consequential. My game group has played it six times in two weeks and we're still discovering new combos. AI Tabletop Co. knocked it out of the park.
