# Kingdoms Ascendant Expansion Pack - Review

**Rating:** ★★★★★
**Reviewer:** Sofia M.
**Date:** April 12, 2026

I backed this on day one and have zero regrets. The solo mode they added in this expansion is phenomenal. The AI opponent deck simulates a real player surprisingly well, and the difficulty scaling means I can ramp it up as I improve. Perfect for weeknights when I can't get the group together. This expansion turned a great multiplayer game into a great anytime game.
