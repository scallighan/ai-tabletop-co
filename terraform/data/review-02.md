# Kingdoms Ascendant Expansion Pack - Review

**Rating:** ★★★★★
**Reviewer:** Priya S.
**Date:** April 2, 2026

The component quality alone is worth the price. The new faction miniatures are incredibly detailed, and the updated resource cards have a linen finish that feels great in your hands. Gameplay-wise, the diplomacy track is a brilliant addition — it completely changes how you negotiate alliances. Highly recommend for any Kingdoms Ascendant fan.
