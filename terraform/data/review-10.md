# Kingdoms Ascendant Expansion Pack - Review

**Rating:** ★★★★★
**Reviewer:** Yuki N.
**Date:** April 15, 2026

Kingdoms Ascendant was already my favorite strategy game, and this expansion just cemented that. The way the new espionage system interacts with the existing political layer creates these tense, delicious moments of betrayal and surprise. I also love that the expansion supports up to 6 players now — game nights with our full group are finally possible. AI Tabletop Co. has earned a customer for life.
