# Kingdoms Ascendant Expansion Pack - Review

**Rating:** ★★★★★
**Reviewer:** Elena V.
**Date:** April 7, 2026

This is exactly the expansion I was hoping for. The two new factions — the Ironveil Collective and the Sunspire Dominion — each have unique play styles that feel balanced but completely distinct. It's rare for an expansion to add this much content without bloating the game. Our average play time only went up by about 20 minutes. Superb design work.
