# Kingdoms Ascendant Expansion Pack - Review

**Rating:** ★★★★★
**Reviewer:** Chris H.
**Date:** April 13, 2026

The rulebook for this expansion deserves special praise — clear, well-organized, with tons of examples. We were up and running in under 15 minutes. The new "Legacy of the Realm" campaign mode gives you 8 linked scenarios with persistent consequences, which adds incredible replayability. We're on scenario 4 and every session has been memorable.
