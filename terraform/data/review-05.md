# Kingdoms Ascendant Expansion Pack - Review

**Rating:** ★★★★★
**Reviewer:** Deshawn R.
**Date:** April 9, 2026

My family has been playing Kingdoms Ascendant every weekend for the past year, and we were starting to settle into predictable strategies. This expansion blew that wide open. The event deck keeps everyone on their toes, and the new trade route system rewards long-term planning in a really satisfying way. Best board game purchase I've made this year.
