# Kingdoms Ascendant Expansion Pack - Review

**Rating:** ★★★★☆
**Reviewer:** Jordan K.
**Date:** April 5, 2026

I was skeptical about adding more complexity to an already deep game, but the expansion integrates seamlessly. The new "Ascension" victory condition gives players who fall behind early a realistic path to winning, which has made our sessions way more competitive down the stretch. Only reason for four stars instead of five is I wish it came with more map tiles.
